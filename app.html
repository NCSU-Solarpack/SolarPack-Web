Dashboard Overview

The main dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of your SolarPack system's performance. Get instant insights into your energy production, consumption, and system health at a glance.

  • Real-time energy production metrics
  • Current system status indicators
  • Quick access to key system controls
  • Weather integration for performance optimization
  • Daily, weekly, and monthly summaries

Data Analytics

Advanced analytics help you understand your energy patterns and optimize your solar pack performance. Dive deep into historical data and predictive insights.

  • Interactive charts and graphs
  • Historical performance trends
  • Energy efficiency recommendations
  • Cost savings calculations
  • Performance comparison tools

System Monitoring

Monitor every aspect of your solar pack system in real-time. Stay informed about performance, maintenance needs, and system optimization opportunities.

  • Live performance monitoring
  • System health diagnostics
  • Maintenance alerts and reminders
  • Component status tracking
  • Remote troubleshooting capabilities

Settings & Control

Take full control of your SolarPack system with comprehensive settings and configuration options. Customize the system to match your specific energy needs and preferences.

  • System configuration settings
  • Energy management preferences
  • Notification and alert customization
  • User account management
  • Device pairing and connectivity

Energy Reports

Generate detailed reports about your energy production, consumption, and savings. Track your environmental impact and financial benefits over time.

  • Customizable report generation
  • Export data in multiple formats
  • Environmental impact tracking
  • Financial savings analysis
  • Scheduled report delivery
